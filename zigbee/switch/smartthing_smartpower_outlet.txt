Smartthings SmartPower Outlet
--------------------------------
Samsung SmartThings Outlet, Works with Amazon Alexa
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


	.. image:: ../../images/switch/smartthing_smartpower_outlet.png
	.. :align: left

Brief information
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	The SmartPower Outlet lets you remotely control lights, electronics, and small appliances. Set your coffee maker to on in the morning or automate your lights to turn on when the sun sets. You can also restrict the power used by fans, lights, and other plug-in appliances.


Inclusion/Exclusion to/from a network
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	#. Put controller to Inclusion/Exclusion mode
	#. Press and hold program button while powering
	#. Release button when LED flashes
	#. Wait for controller completes inclusion/exclusion process
